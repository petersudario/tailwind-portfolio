About

It all started in 2013 when I started to mess the code from the game called "Minecraft", giving birth to the passion that I have on programming.

In 2020, I started to learn programming in the last years of high school (they offered courses along with normal education), took the "Computer Technician" certificate in 2022 and, in 2023, started to get a degree of "Bachelor in Information Systems" on PUCPR University, Brazil.

At the start of 2024, I joined as Founding President from the Information Systems Academic Center at PUCPR, to provide assistance to students and teachers, and to promote events around the campus. Near the start of the second semester of 2024, I also joined as the 2nd Secretary of Central Student Directory in PUCPR, providing assistance to all students from the Curitiba Campus and other campuses around the state (like Londrina and Toledo cities). And finally, nearing the end of 2024, I joined as the General Secretary from PUCPR Academic Athletic Association of Computing (A.A.A.C - OCTACORE), providing further assistance to the association's board and members. All of these 3 entities includes a lot of work with the student body, teachers and the university itself, to provide a better experience for everyone involved, creating events, encourage students to participate in academic events and spread knowledge from their courses, and to provide assistance to students that are in need of help on their careers or studies.

Now moving out of the academic path: In 2023, I started to work at Inuar Technology as a PHP Intern Developer, creating modules, migrating databases and maintaining projects with Symfony based frameworks.

On mid 2023, I left Inuar Technology and started to work on a french multinational company called "Sagemcom", as a Intern Research & Development Support Analyst, creating validation tests for the company's products.

Jumping to August 2024, I left the Developer's team and moved to the QA Team in Sagemcom, to provide support as a QA Analyst, creating test cases, test plans, validate products, analyze bugs and provide support to company's customers. Despite the change, I kept evolving the validation libraries and the test cases that I created in the Developer's team, as a part-time QA Engineer.

Even with all of these events happening, I still have time to work on personal projects and freelance jobs with friends and contacts, to keep my skills sharp and to learn new technologies and methods that I can use in my daily work.

I love reading classic horror such as Edgar Allan Poe and H.P Lovecraft , manga comics, Sci-fi like "Bladerunner - Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheeps?" and mythlogy books like "Norse Mythology from Neil Gaiman".

I also love movies, like Alien, Guardians of the Galaxy, Star Wars, Oppenheimer, Interstellar, Dune and superhero movies.

And as a hobby, I love playing games and to listen to music.
