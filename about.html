About

It all started in 2013 when I began tinkering with the code of the game "Minecraft," igniting my passion for programming.

In 2020, I delved deeper into programming during my final years of high school, earning a "Computer Technician" certificate in 2022. By 2023, I commenced my "Bachelor in Information Systems" at PUCPR University, Brazil.

In 2024, I took on leadership roles such as Founding President of the Information Systems Academic Center and 2nd Secretary of the Central Student Directory at PUCPR. Additionally, I became the General Secretary of the PUCPR Academic Athletic Association of Computing (A.A.A.C - OCTACORE), contributing significantly to student engagement and academic growth.

Professionally, I have experience as a PHP Intern Developer at Inuar Technology and as a QA Analyst at Sagemcom, where I honed my skills in creating test cases, validating products, and providing customer support.

Beyond academics and work, I actively engage in personal projects and freelance work to continually refine my skills and embrace new technologies.

I have a deep love for classic horror literature, manga comics, sci-fi, mythology books, movies, gaming, and music, which fuels my creativity and problem-solving abilities.
